Toilet Units:

Supplied in both timber and steel units, Ultrakabin can offer you a wide range of portable toilet units. These units can also incorporate changing rooms and shower facilities. Our units are designed for easy connection to half inch mains and four inch plastic sewage pipe outlets. Ultrakabin portable toilet units are fitted out to the highest specification, providing you with clean, easy to maintain, warm facilities

  • .7 Colourcoated Steel Exterior
  • .7 Washable Steel Interior
  • 18MM WBP Playwood Flooring
  • Vinyl Floor Covering
  • 4ft fluorescent & Bulkhead lighting
  • Under Sink Water Heater
  • Vinyl Placed Plywood Ceilings
