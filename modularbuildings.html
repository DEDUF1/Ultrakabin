Modular Buildings:

Ultrakabin Modular Building offer all the comfort and finish of a permanent structure, at an affordable price. We can rapidly install office accommodation to any level of luxury finish on your site and make it fully operational within weeks

  • Decorative Internal Finish
  • Suspended Ceilings
  • Inset Lighting
  • Double Glazed Windows
  • Carpet Tiles
  • Toilet and Wash Room Facilities
  • Ducting and Communication Systems
