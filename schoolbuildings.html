School Buildings:

Ultrakabin units are Department of education approved for use as classroom and facilities for schools. Whatever your needs are, from a small ten place classroom to a large complex of classrooms, Ultrakabin have the experience and expertise to provide you with the building you are looking for.

Ultrakabin can undertake to install and finish contracts including all site work and connections. Our staff will work closely with you to incorporate any of your particular needs in our flexible design

Standard Features Include:
  • Double Glazed Windows
  • Electrical Fittings and Appliances
  • Washroom Facilities
  • Toilets
  • Hat & Coat Hooks
  • Blackboards
  • Carpet Tiles
  • 2.5 mm Vinyl Floor Covering
